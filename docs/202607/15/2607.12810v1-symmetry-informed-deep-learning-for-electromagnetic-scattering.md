## 摘要
深度学习可以通过使用训练好的神经网络将设计参数映射到散射参数，从而取代昂贵的数值模拟，加速电磁器件的建模。然而，由于训练数据通常是通过昂贵的数值模拟生成的，数据效率仍然是一个核心瓶颈。在这里，我们展示了对称性为克服电磁散射问题中的这一限制提供了一条强大且在很大程度上尚未被开发的途径。利用麦克斯韦方程组的等变性，我们获得了通用的变换规则，将电磁器件的对称性映射到其散射参数的相应变换。这使得系统性的数据增强和精确等变神经网络的构建成为可能。我们针对离散和连续对称群实现了该框架，并在光子晶体板和自由形状衍射光栅上证明了其有效性。与标准架构相比，引入对称性将数据效率提高了一个数量级，而等变模型还额外精确地执行了物理约束。我们的方法具有通用性，且与现有的物理信息策略互补，为构建具有物理依据的代理模型提供了一个第一性原理框架，并将对称性确立为计算电磁学及其他领域中实现高效数据和物理一致性学习的统一归纳偏置。

## Abstract
Deep learning can accelerate the modeling of electromagnetic devices by replacing costly simulations with neural networks trained to map design parameters to scattering parameters. However, data efficiency remains a central bottleneck, as training data is typically generated through expensive numerical simulations. Here we show that symmetry provides a powerful and largely untapped route to overcoming this limitation in electromagnetic scattering problems. Leveraging the equivariance of Maxwell's equations, we obtain general transformation rules that map symmetries of electromagnetic devices to corresponding transformations of their scattering parameters. This enables both systematic data augmentation and the construction of exactly equivariant neural networks. We implement the framework for both discrete and continuous symmetry groups and demonstrate its effectiveness on photonic-crystal slabs and free-form diffraction gratings. Incorporating symmetry improves data efficiency by an order of magnitude compared to standard architectures, while equivariant models additionally enforce physical constraints exactly. Our approach is general and complementary to existing physics-informed strategies, provides a first-principles framework for constructing physically grounded surrogate models, and establishes symmetry as a unifying inductive bias for data-efficient and physically consistent learning in computational electromagnetics and beyond.
